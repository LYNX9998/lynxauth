Pricing Questions, Answered

Everything most users ask before choosing a plan.

Can I switch from Free to a paid plan later?

Yes. You can start on the free plan, build your app, and upgrade to Silver or Gold whenever you need more applications, support, or advanced access.

Does the currency switch change the features?

No. The INR and USD toggle only changes how the yearly price is displayed. Every plan keeps the same features and limits.

Gold Developer is the strongest choice for large-scale or professional usage because it includes unlimited apps and users, source access, private database support, and dedicated help.
